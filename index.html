Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services: Firebase (Google): For user authentication and account management only. We do not store your readings in Firebase. Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication with secure protocols to protect your account No Audio Recording: Text-to-speech functionality processes text locally and does not record or transmit audio 6.
